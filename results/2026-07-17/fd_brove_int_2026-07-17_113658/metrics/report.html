Goal success (re-scored) = deterministic rule: normalized bot decision equals the scripted expectation AND the quoted amount matches (currency-aware, ±2%). Terminal decision = bot rendered a final determination rather than stalling or escalating. Clean runs = harness completed without a runtime error (a test-agent metric, not a bot metric).
